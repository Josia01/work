Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from hotel owners, investors, and developers.

We work across boutique hotels, full-service hotels, luxury resorts, spa and wellness retreats, mixed-use developments, serviced apartments, and standalone restaurant and F&B operations. We have particular expertise in niche and independent properties that benefit most from specialist management rather than branded chains.
Our headquarters and primary operations are in Mombasa, Kenya, and we have deep local expertise across the Kenyan coastal and urban markets. However, we have consulted on projects across East Africa and leverage our UNWTO partnership to bring global market intelligence to every engagement.
We offer hybrid management contracts that are highly linked to outcomes with fully transparent cost structures. Rather than flat fees regardless of performance, our model aligns our incentives directly with your property's success — we do well when you do well. Specific terms are tailored to each engagement.

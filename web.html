Consulting Services

Six stages. One strategic thread, start to finish.

We collaborate with the UNWTO (World Tourism Organization) to keep every recommendation grounded in current global market trends.

01

Market & Feasibility Analysis

Competition studies, in-depth feasibility & market research, identification of new trends and needs, and analysis of socio-economic and socio-cultural values to shape concept development and positioning.

02

Business Plan & Project Development

Financing research and investor introductions, project concept development and adaptation of existing plans, and advice on franchise versus independent management — including F&B concept repositioning.

03

Pre-Opening Support

Positioning strategy and sales & marketing plans, supplier selection across FF&E, OS&E and IT, staff recruitment & training, and support designing the corporate image and website.

04

Project Management

Snagging control, full project coordination, quality control through construction, and close coordination with operators, technical teams and equipment suppliers through handover.

05

Operational Support & Advice

Ongoing quality control, web design consulting, sales & marketing analysis, and revenue & e-commerce management to keep distribution channels performing.

06

Training

Student placements and internal hotel training, delivered alongside academic partners and our ongoing collaboration with the UNWTO.
